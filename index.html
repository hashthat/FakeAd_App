How Our AI Detection Works

We use multi-modal machine learning, pattern matching, and continuous feedback to identify misleading advertising across text, image and video.

Multi-Modal Analysis

  • Text: NLP flags exaggerated language and unsupported claims.
  • Image: Forensics detect manipulation and misleading comparisons.
  • Video: Frame-by-frame checks reveal edits and inconsistencies.
